Who We Are......

BattleBorn Anesthesia is physicians owned single-specialty, anesthesia practice in Nevada, where excellence in anesthesia care meets unwavering commitment to patient safety and comfort. Founded with a vision to render patient centered quality care, we specialize in providing safe anesthesia services tailored to meet the unique needs of each patient and need of healthcare facilities where we serve. Our name meets our motto” To keep fighting for what we believe.

During any surgery, our anesthesologists are more than happy to assit the patients and walk them through the intricate and sensitive process:

  • Preoperative Assessment and Consultation.
  • Anesthesia Administration.
  • Postoperative Care.

“At Battleborn Anesthesia, our mission is to blend precision with compassion, ensuring every patient’s journey is as comfortable and seamless as possible. We’re dedicated to making every procedure a testament to our commitment to excellence and care.”


Our Services

Consultation

Working closely with surgeons, nurses, and other medical professionals to develop and implement anesthesia plans and ensure overall patient safety.

Anesthesia Services

Administering anesthesia during surgical procedures, which includes general anesthesia, regional anesthesia (like epidurals or spinal blocks), and sedation

Education and Training

Offering education and training for hospital staff on anesthesia practices and safety protocols.

Critical Care Service

Offering expertise in the management of critically ill patients, including those in intensive care units (ICUs), where advanced monitoring and life support may be necessary.
